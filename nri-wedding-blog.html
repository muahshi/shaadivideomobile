NRI Wedding Ki Asli Problem Kya Hai?

Zyada tar NRIs yeh sochte hain ki problem hai paisa ya distance. Lekin actually problem hai — coordination ka bharosa.

Jab tum 5,000 miles door ho, toh har cheez ek chain mein hoti hai. Ek vendor ne call nahi uthaya toh agla step ruk jaata hai. Ek payment delay hui toh venue booking cancel ho sakti hai. Aur tum kuch kar nahi sakte — bas WhatsApp par message karo aur wait karo.

📌 NRI Couples Ke Top 3 Dard-Points
  • Vendor ne rate quote kiya — phir baat karte karte double kar diya
  • Family ne haan bol diya kisi cheez ke liye jo tumhe pasand nahi thi
  • Koi bhi decision tumhare bina nahi hota, toh tum 24/7 available rehte ho

Yeh problems solvable hain. Bas ek system chahiye — aur thodi AI ki madad.

5 Galtiyan Jo NRIs Commonly Karte Hain

1
Kisi ek family member par sab depend karna
Ek coordinator enough nahi hota. Ek "primary" aur ek "backup" rakhna. Agar primary ki tabiyat kharab hui toh kya hoga?
2
Verbal agreements par chalte rehna
Har cheez — rate, date, menu, cancellation policy — email ya WhatsApp par likhi honi chahiye. Vendor se bolna: "Bhai, ek baar WhatsApp par confirm kar do."
3
Buffer budget nahi rakhna
Remote planning mein surprises aate hain. Minimum 10–15% buffer rakhna. Nikhil ne rakha tha — ₹4 lakh — aur ₹1.9 lakh wapas gaye.
4
Har chhoti cheez approve karwana
Time barbad hota hai. Kuch boundaries set karo: "Family ₹20,000 tak khud decide kar sakti hai."
5
Alag alag tools use karna — koi integration nahi
Spreadsheet mein guest list, alag app mein budget, dimaag mein timeline — yeh combination disaster hai. Smart Shaadi ka free dashboard sab integrate karta hai.
